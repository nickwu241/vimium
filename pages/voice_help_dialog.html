Navigation

Moving around page: Say a direction such as Up, Down, Left or Right.

Clicking links: Say Links to toggle on all the possible link navigation on the current screen. Then say Go To [LETTER] to click the link.

Go back: Go Back.

Go forward: Go Forward.

Pro Tip

Keep scrolling by specifying keep in your command and stop to stop.

Control the amount of movement by specifying a little or a page.

For recipes, you can say Go To Ingredients to see the required ingredients and Go To Instructions to see the cooking instructions.

Close help page: Close Help
